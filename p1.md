##  3. ATM-Machine Project

* * *

A project that simulates the functionality of an Automated Teller Machine (ATM). It allows users to perform various financial transactions conveniently.

### 🌟 Features


#### 🚀 User Authentication


- **Get Started**: 🌟 Begin by entering your unique ID and PIN.
- **Oops, Wrong PIN?**: 😬 If you happen to enter the wrong PIN, don't sweat it! You have three chances to get it right. 🎯


#### 💰 Available Options


Choose from a variety of exciting options:


- **Balance Check**: 💰 Instantly view your account balance and watch your wealth grow! 📈
- **Transaction History**: 📜 Access a detailed record of your recent transactions and track your financial journey.
- **Withdraw**: 💸 Easily withdraw cash from your account, because sometimes you need those crispy bills! 💵
- **Deposit**: 💳 Safely deposit funds into your account and watch your savings grow. 🌱
- **Transfer**: 🔄 Swiftly transfer money between accounts and help your loved ones when they need it most.
- **Quit**: 🏁 When you're done, exit the ATM interface with a single click and proceed with your day. 


#### ⌛ Seamless Operations



Select an option, and watch the magic unfold. Our project empowers you to complete your transactions quickly and efficiently, making banking a breeze. ✨


### 🛠️ Tools and Technologies



Explore the technologies that power our ATM Machine Project:


- 💡 **Core Java**: The foundation of our project, providing the essential structure and functionality.

- 🌐 **JDBC (Java Database Connectivity)**: Seamlessly connecting our project to the database, ensuring secure data handling.

- 🗄️ **MySQL**: The reliable and robust database system where your financial information is stored safely and efficiently.

##  4. BMI Calculator

* * *

<p align="center">
   <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/125716338/229718921-4983f9f0-2fab-4f3c-9ccc-6a034c479cbd.png" alt="Project Screenshot" width="600">

<h3 align="center">🧮  BMI Calculator</h3>


The BMI Calculator is a user-friendly tool that enables users to quickly assess their health by calculating their Body Mass Index. It's built with Java and JavaFX for a seamless and interactive experience.

### 🌟 Features

- **User-Friendly Interface**:
   - An intuitive and easy-to-use interface for a hassle-free experience.
  
- **Input Fields**:
   - Input your weight in kilograms and your height in meters to provide the necessary data for BMI calculation.

- **Calculation**:
   - Click the "GO" button to trigger the BMI calculation.

- **BMI Calculation**:
   - The calculator performs the BMI calculation using your weight and height.

- **Display BMI Index**:
   - The resulting BMI index is displayed, allowing you to see your calculated BMI value.

- **Health Assessment**:
   - The BMI index helps assess whether your weight falls within a healthy range, underweight, overweight, or obese, based on standard BMI categories.

### 🛠️ Tools and Technologies

- ☕ Java 
- 🚀 JavaFX
